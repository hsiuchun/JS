Reinforcement learning

Reinforcement Learning (RL) is a type of machine learning technique that enables an agent to learn in an interactive environment by trial and error using feedback from its own actions and experiences.

Q & A

  • What is MDP?

    The problem that MDP wants to deal with is when the action you take will not reach the state you want exactly as you expected, how do you take the action?
    Known as : (State S, Action a, Transition T, Reward R,Gamma γ)

  • How to solve MDP?

    Bellman Equation include value iteration/policy iteration, policy evaluation and policy extraction. Using policy iteration will lead to more faster convergency than using value iteration.

  • What are difference between On-policy and Off-policy learning?

    Compared with MDP is Offline, RL is Online. Three kinds of ways for solving RL introduced here are MC, TD, and Q-learning. On-policy means that the sampling π is the same with update one, on the contrary, Off-policy means they are not the same one.

  • What are difference between MC,TD,and Q-learning?

    The value updating of MC will compute at the end of the episode, on the contrary, TD will update at the certain step(s), and Q-learning will upadte at every time step. In addtionally, the reward computing are also different from them.
